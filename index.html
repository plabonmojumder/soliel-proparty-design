What is a bridge engineer?

What is a bridge engineer? An engineer is an engineer who acts as a bridge between the customer's company (Japanese company) and offshore development (consignment to overseas) with engineers living in Bangladesh. Our bridge engineers are stationed at the Yokohama Development Center and use e-mail and zoom to manage the progress of your project. Bridge engineers recognize the differences in language, culture, and monetary value between Japan and Bangladesh, and provide coordination and consulting services between your company and Bangladeshi IT engineers.
